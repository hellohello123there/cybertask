Our Security Measures

To protect against future cyber-attacks, we have implemented the following security measures:

  • Regular system updates to patch vulnerabilities.
  • Firewall configuration to block unauthorised access.
  • Encrypted backups to prevent data loss.
  • Two-factor authentication (2FA) for all administrative accounts.
